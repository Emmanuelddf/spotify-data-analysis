
# Spotify Data Analysis – Key Insights

## 1. Track Duration
- Most popular tracks are between 3–3.5 minutes.
- Shorter (<2.5 min) or longer (>5 min) tracks generally have lower popularity.

## 2. Popularity by Decade
- Tracks released in the 2010s dominate Spotify popularity metrics.
- Popularity has generally increased over the years.

## 3. Album and Artist Performance
- Certain albums consistently have higher average track popularity.
- Top artists include HUNTR/X, Saja boys, Disco lines, Olivia Dean, and Rumi, whose tracks frequently appear in top charts.

## 4. Other Observations
- There is a moderate positive correlation between track duration and popularity.
- Outliers exist: a few long-duration tracks (>6 min) still achieve very high popularity.
